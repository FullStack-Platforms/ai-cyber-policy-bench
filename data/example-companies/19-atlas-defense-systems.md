### Atlas Defense Systems

**Organization Overview:**
* **Business Details:** Defense contractor manufacturing electronic warfare systems, radar components, and communication equipment for military applications. Produces specialized hardware for aerospace and defense customers with security clearance requirements.
* **Industry:** Government/Defense - Defense Manufacturing
* **Size:** 750-950 employees, $200-320M revenue
* **Structure:** Public corporation, ITAR-registered, cleared facility operations
* **Technology Infrastructure:** Classified and unclassified networks, CAD/engineering systems, manufacturing execution systems, government cloud environments (IL4/IL5)

**Compliance Requirements:**
* **Applicable Frameworks/Regulations:**
    * CMMC Level 2
    * NIST 800-171 Rev. 3
    * NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0
    * ISO 27001

**Policy Parameters:**
* **Critical Constraints:** ITAR compliance for defense articles, export control regulations, classified contract requirements
* **Key Stakeholders:** Facility Security Officer, ITAR Compliance Manager, Manufacturing Security, Cleared Personnel
* **Sensitive Systems/Data:** Technical data packages, manufacturing specifications, classified design information
